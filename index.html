Welcome to Pets vs Skeletons!

The rules are very simple. There are skeletons coming to destroy your home! Luckily, your pets have been training for this day and ready to defend you! All you have to do is place your pets in any of the available grid spaces in the three leftmost columns (yellow when highlighted).

One thing to remember, however, is that you need to pay to place your pets! They cost 50 points each and that comes from your score so if you place too many too fast you will run out so be frugal! Defeating skeletons earn you some points back though so be strategic but beware of Skelly Plants that sometimes spawn when you defeat a Skeleton. They shoot blasts too!

That's pretty much it so give it your best shot and beat those boneheads!
